Some of the Awesome Math in Genesis 1:1


Bible Math Facts
Genesis 1:1 In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth.
Genesis 1:1 in Hebrew is בראשית ברא אלהים את השמים ואת הארץ has a standard Hebrew count of characters equal to 2701.
The prime factors of 2701 are 37 and 73, in other words, 37x73 is equal to 2701.
Chochma (חכמה) is the Hebrew Word for wisdom. Adding the Hebrew ordinal values of each Hebrew character in 'חכמה' equals 73.
By taking each character for the Hebrew word for wisdom 'הםכח' and add up the ordinal value for each character you get 37.
37 is the 12th prime number and 73 is the 21st prime number. Note 12 and 21 are mirror images.
The mirror value of 2701 is 1072. When you add (2701+1072) you get 3773.
Genesis 1:1 in Hebrew using Mispar Shemi gematria (e.g. letter counting) equals 5476, which is equal to 74 squared. e.g. 74x74.
Adding the positions of each letter of 'JESUS' equals 74. J=10, E=5, S=19, U=21, S=19.
74 is the 37th even number.
